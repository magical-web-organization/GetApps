The solution also provides intrusion prevention capabilities that detect and block attempts to exploit vulnerabilities in the network infrastructure or applications.

### 4. Device control

SEP allows organizations to control and monitor the use of removable devices, such as USB drives, to prevent data leakage and theft.

SEP provides a centralized management console that enables IT administrators to easily deploy, manage, and monitor the security of endpoints across the organization.
