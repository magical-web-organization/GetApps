## What is Dribbble?
Dribbble is a web-based platform where designers showcase their work in the form of shots, GIFs, animations, and designs. Founded in 2009 by Dan Cederholm and Rich Thornett, Dribbble now has over 6 million users and continues to grow. The platform's ethos is to provide designers with a forum to share their work with other designers, seek constructive feedback, and get inspiration from others.

## Features of Dribbble
Dribbble offers a wide range of features that benefit designers. Some of the most notable features of Dribbble include:

- **Showcase your work:** Dribbble allows you to upload your projects and share them with the world. You can add a description to your shots, let people know what inspired you, and detail the process behind designing the work.
- **Find Work:** Dribbble provides designers with a job board that showcases the latest jobs available for designers. You can filter by location, type of job, and job function.
- **Community Collaboration:** You can connect with other designers and build your network on Dribbble. The platform allows you to follow other designers, leave comments, and get inspiration from other designers' works.
- **Groups and Teams:** Dribbble enables designers to create groups or join teams based on specific design niches or interests. Being part of a group or team allows you to engage with like-minded designers, share work, and receive feedback.
