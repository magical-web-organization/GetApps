## GnuCash: An Overview

GnuCash is a free and open-source personal and small-business financial-accounting software. It is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux-based systems. This software allows users to manage their finances by tracking bank accounts, stocks, income, and expenses.

## GnuCash as a Bookkeeping Ledger Tool

One of the features of GnuCash that users find helpful is its bookkeeping functionality. GnuCash allows users to create a bookkeeping ledger that they can use to record their financial transactions. To create a new ledger, users need to open GnuCash and select "New" from the "File" menu. They can then choose "New Book" and give the ledger a name.

Once a ledger is created, users can start recording their financial transactions by adding accounts. They can add different types of accounts, such as assets, liabilities, income, and expenses. Users can also create sub-accounts and categories to help them organize transactions better.
