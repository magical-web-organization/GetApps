## What is PowerPom?

PowerPom is a Pomodoro Timer software that allows you to work on tasks in specific intervals, typically 25 minutes, followed by a short break. This technique is called the Pomodoro Technique and is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.

With PowerPom, you can customize the duration of the work session and break time according to your preferences. After completing a set of work intervals, you can take a longer break to recharge before resuming the work cycle.

## How Does PowerPom Work?

Using PowerPom is simple and straightforward. Once you open the software, you can set the desired work and break intervals, and begin the work cycle. PowerPom will remind you when it's time to take a break, and when the break is over, it will prompt you to resume work.

PowerPom also has a feature that tracks your progress and shows you how many cycles you've completed. This feature can help you stay motivated and accountable, and provide a sense of accomplishment as you see your work add up over time.
