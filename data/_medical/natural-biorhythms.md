## What are Biorhythms?

Biorhythms refer to the cyclical patterns that govern our physical, emotional, and intellectual states. These patterns were first introduced by Wilhelm Fliess, a German physician, in the late 19th century. According to Fliess, our bodies follow a series of cycles that last roughly 23, 28, and 33 days, respectively. These cycles are believed to impact our moods, energy levels, and cognitive abilities, among other things.
